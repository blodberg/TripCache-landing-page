# Privacy Policy
We value your privacy. TripCache does not collect, transmit or distribute your data.


<br>

## Data Storage
Your data is stored locally on your device and in your iCloud account (if enabled). We do not store any information from users — no names, no email addresses, no location data.


<br>

## Analytics
Beyond the optional anonymous debugging information provided by Apple, TripCache does not use any analytics tools or tracking technologies.



<br>

## Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns, you can contact us at: support@tripcache.app
