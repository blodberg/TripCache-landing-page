# Privacy Policy
TripCache and its developer take your privacy very seriously. TripCache does not collect, transmit or distribute your data.


<br>

## Data Storage
Your data is stored locally on your device and in your iCloud account (if enabled). TripCache does not store any information from users — no names, no email addresses, no location data.


<br>

## Analytics
Beyond the optional anonymous debugging information provided by Apple, TripCache does not use any analytics tools, tracking technologies or ad frameworks.



<br>

## Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about any of the above, please contact <support@tripcache.app>
