Log travel expenses with a few taps. TripCache handles the currencies, the math, and the report, so you can get back to the trip.

Features

  1. Your trip at a glance

    Open a trip and the numbers that matter are right at the top: daily average and running total. Expenses group by day with daily totals underneath, so you can see exactly where the money went.

  2. Any currency, zero math

    Pesos at lunch, dollars by dinner. Every expense converts back to your home currency automatically, so totals always make sense. Exchange rates are cached before you leave, which means conversions keep working even when your connection doesn't.

  3. Works offline, stays private

    Everything works offline and syncs through your iCloud when you're back. Your spending data lives on your device: no accounts, no servers of ours, no analytics peeking at your purchases.

  4. Find anything, fast

    Search across every trip and every expense from one place. Clear category icons keep long trips easy to scan.
